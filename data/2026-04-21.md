Discussing the similarities and differences among these nickel oxides will provide new insights into understanding the mechanism of high-Tc superconductivity in correlated electron systems. In this paper, we review the experimental and theoretical progress on RuddlesdenPopper nickelates, with emphasis on thin films, and discuss future perspectives and research directions.
