### [1] [Altermagnets Enable Gate-Switchable Helical and Chiral Topological Transport with Spin-Valley-Momentum-Locked Dual Protection](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06487)
*Xianzhang Chen,Jiayong Zhang,Bowen Hao,Jiahui Qian,Ziye Zhu,Igor Zutic,Zhenyu Zhang,Tong Zhou*

Main category: cond-mat.mes-hall

TL;DR: 该研究建立了一个基于交替磁体的统一对称性框架，结合谷拓扑实现螺旋和手性拓扑相的电控转换，为自旋-谷-动量锁定的拓扑器件提供了可编程平台。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 探索交替磁体中的对称性驱动拓扑相，实现螺旋和手性拓扑相在单一材料平台中的电控转换，为自旋电子学和谷电子学提供可编程的拓扑器件平台。

Method: 1. 建立对称性驱动的统一框架，结合交替磁体的交替自旋劈裂和谷拓扑；2. 理论证明交替磁体中的磁类比量子自旋霍尔效应，产生螺旋自旋-谷-动量锁定边缘态；3. 大规模量子输运模拟验证拓扑保护特性；4. 引入栅极可调子晶格交错势，选择性打开谷隙实现螺旋到手性相的转换；5. 第一性原理计算识别V2STeO和VO单层材料作为实验平台。

Result: 1. 交替磁体中实现螺旋自旋-谷-动量锁定边缘态，具有复合自旋-谷陈数Csv=2；2. 量子输运模拟显示完全量子化的自旋电导，对非磁性和长程磁无序具有鲁棒性；3. 栅极可调势能实现螺旋相到手性量子反常霍尔相的转换（Csv=1）；4. 反转势能可切换传输的自旋-谷极化；5. 识别V2STeO和VO单层材料为可行的实验平台。

Conclusion: 交替磁体可作为电可编程平台，支持螺旋和手性拓扑相之间的电控转换，为电荷、自旋和谷自由度的鲁棒拓扑器件提供了新途径，具有重要的自旋电子学和谷电子学应用前景。

Abstract: We establish a unified, symmetry-driven framework that combines the alternating spin splitting of altermagnets with valley topology to realize and electrically interconvert helical and chiral topological phases within a single material platform. We first demonstrate a magnetic analogue of the quantum spin Hall effect in altermagnets, hosting helical spin-valley-momentum-locked (SVML) edge states characterized by a composite spin-valley Chern number Csv = 2. Large-scale quantum transport simulations show these SVML edge states exhibit fully quantized spin conductance robust against nonmagnetic and long-range magnetic disorder, reflecting their dual topological protection, while remaining vulnerable to short-range magnetic disorder. Exploiting that the counterpropagating SVML modes are linked by crystal rotation symmetry, we introduce a gate-tunable sublattice-staggered potential that selectively gaps one valley and converts the helical state into a chiral quantum anomalous Hall phase with Csv = 1, robust against all disorder types. Reversing the potential switches the transmitted spin-valley polarization. Our first-principles calculations identify monolayer V2STeO and VO families as realistic platforms supporting both helical and chiral topological phases and their electrical switching. These results establish altermagnets as electrically programmable platforms for robust topological devices across charge, spin, and valley.

### [2] [Nanoscale Electronic Phase Separation Driven by Fe-site Ordering in Fe\textsubscript{5-x}GeTe\textsubscript{2}](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06539)
*Shreyashi Sinha,Ayan Jana,Suchanda Mondal,Ravi Prakash Singh,Manoranjan Kumar,Sujit Manna*

Main category: cond-mat.mes-hall

TL;DR: 该研究通过扫描隧道显微镜和密度泛函理论，揭示了Fe5-xGeTe2中Fe位点有序化如何驱动电子相分离，导致金属性和赝隙电子态在纳米尺度共存。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 理解局部结构有序如何调控电子关联是揭示二维磁体涌现行为微观机制的关键。Fe5-xGeTe2中固有的Fe位点无序为探究这种相互作用提供了天然平台。

Method: 结合高分辨率扫描隧道显微镜（STM）和密度泛函理论（DFT）计算，在原子尺度建立Fe位点有序化与局部电子结构的直接关联。

Result: STM观测到两种共存表面相：√3×√3超结构（有序Fe(1)构型）和未畸变1×1六方Te晶格（Fe(1)缺失区）。√3有序区域呈金属性，而Fe(1)空缺区在费米能级附近显示抑制态密度，呈现赝隙电子态。DFT计算表明Fe 3d与Te 5p轨道对称允许的杂化重构了低能电子结构。

Conclusion: Fe位点有序化驱动的电子相分离导致金属性和赝隙电子态在纳米尺度共存，为理解范德华磁体中原子尺度结构有序与纳米尺度电子不均匀性之间的联系提供了微观框架。

Abstract: Understanding how local structural order governs electronic correlations is essential for revealing the microscopic mechanism underlying emergent behavior in two-dimensional magnets. In the layered van der Waals ferromagnet Fe\textsubscript{5-x}GeTe\textsubscript{2}, intrinsic Fe-site disorder provides a natural platform to probe this interplay. Here, we establish a direct atomic scale correlation between Fe-site ordering and local electronic structure by combining high-resolution scanning tunneling microscopy with density functional theory calculations. Scanning tunneling microscopy resolves two coexisting surface phases, a $\sqrt{3} \times \sqrt{3}$ superstructure associated with ordered Fe(1) configurations and an undistorted $1 \times 1$ hexagonal Te lattice in Fe(1)-deficient regions. Spatially resolved spectroscopy shows that the $\sqrt{3}$-ordered domains exhibit metallic behavior, whereas Fe(1) vacant areas display a suppressed density of states(DOS) near the Fermi level, indicative of pseudogapped electronic states. The nanoscale coexistence of these distinct electronic responses provides direct evidence of electronic phase separation driven by Fe-site ordering. First-principles calculations reveal that symmetry allowed hybridization between Fe 3d and Te 5p orbitals reconstructs the low-energy electronic structure, giving rise to the contrasting tunneling signatures of ordered and disordered phases. Bias-dependent local DOS simulations reproduce the experimentally observed contrast evolution and reveal that hybridization induced out of plane orbital character governs the spatial modulation of tunneling conductance. These results provide a microscopic framework linking atomic-scale structural order to nanoscale electronic inhomogeneity in van der Waals magnets.

### [3] [Higher-Order Approximation of Coherent State Dynamics in Self-Interacting Quantum Field Theories](https://arxiv.org/abs/2603.06026)
*Zied Ammari,Julien Malartre,Maher Zerzeri*

Main category: math-ph

TL;DR: 该论文研究了自相互作用玻色子量子场论中相干态的传播，在半经典（平均场）区域构建了量子演化的任意阶渐近展开。


<details>
  <summary>Details</summary>
Motivation: 研究自相互作用量子场论中相干态的传播行为，特别是在半经典区域，旨在理解量子场动力学与经典场动力学之间的关系，并改进先前仅识别展开式领头阶的结果。

Method: 采用Hepp方法，结合对相关经典和量子场动力学的详细分析（分别为非线性和线性），首先在空间截断的P(φ)₂模型下建立结果，然后扩展到一类非多项式解析相互作用。

Result: 成功构建了相干态量子演化的任意阶渐近展开，该结果在标准假设下成立，确保了哈密顿量的本质自伴性和经典流的适定性。

Conclusion: 该工作改进并推广了先前仅识别展开式领头阶的结果，为自相互作用量子场论中相干态的半经典演化提供了更完整的数学描述。

Abstract: We study the propagation of coherent states in self-interacting bosonic quantum field theories in the semi-classical (mean-field) regime. Relying on Hepp's method and a detailed analysis of the associated classical and quantum field dynamics, non-linear and linear respectively, we construct an asymptotic expansion of arbitrary order for the quantum evolution of coherent states. The results are first established for the spatially cutoff $P(φ)_2$ model, under standard assumptions ensuring essential self-adjointness of the Hamiltonian and well-posedness of the classical flow, and are then extended to a class of non-polynomial analytic interactions. This work refines and generalizes earlier results, which identified only the leading-order term of the expansion.
